# Moodboard — references

The cover must sit naturally next to these and feel like it belongs.

## Shelf neighbours (the bar)
- **Atomic Habits** — clean, confident, typography leads.
- **Thinking, Fast and Slow** — intelligent restraint.
- **The Innovator's Dilemma** — serious business classic.
- **Stripe Press** (whole catalogue) — premium, considered, editorial.

## Feeling
calm · intelligent · premium · restrained · contemporary-but-not-trendy ·
confident-without-trying-too-hard · "serious book of ideas," not a SaaS landing page.

## Anti-references (see `design-principles.md` for the full avoid-list)
generic AI/startup design · neon/cyberpunk · literal brains/AI clichés · stock business
graphics (bulbs, gears, rockets, up-arrows) · self-published tells (text shadows,
bevels, gradient type).
