# Typography — choices, rationale, licenses

Hard rules: **≤ 2 families**, serif-led title, **title case** (not all caps), no default
fonts, **free/OFL only**.

## Candidates

**Title (serif, leads):**
- **Fraunces** (variable, high-contrast "old-style" with optical sizes) — intelligent,
  warm, distinctive without shouting. Primary candidate.
- Alternates: Newsreader, Source Serif 4, Spectral, Libre Caslon.

**Subtitle + author:**
- Same serif in another weight/optical size, **or** one restrained grotesque:
  **Inter** / IBM Plex Sans (quiet, editorial, pairs cleanly with a contrast serif).

## Pairing under test (round 1)
- Fraunces (title) + Inter (subtitle/author).

## Licenses
- **Fraunces** — SIL Open Font License 1.1. Free for commercial use.
- **Inter** — SIL Open Font License 1.1. Free for commercial use.

License texts are stored alongside the font files in `assets/fonts/`.
